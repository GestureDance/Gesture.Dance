GAMEPLAY HIGHLIGHTS

What makes Gesture.Dance unique

A VR hand-gesture rhythm experience with gesture matching, gesture-dance combos, a Chinese-style music library, and built-in recording for creating your own tracks.

  1. 01

    Core Gameplay: Dance Through Your Gestures

    Step into an immersive Chinese-style VR world where a distant dancer guides your moves. Various hand-shaped icons will fly toward you, and you simply match each one with the correct hand gesture to clear it. Sometimes they appear one by one, sometimes as flowing gesture chains that you must follow smoothly. Avoid incoming bombs, match the gestures, and as you do so, you naturally perform the full gesture-dance without needing to consciously mimic the dancer—your actions become the choreography.

  2. 02

    A Variety of Gestures, Endless Combinations

    The game features multiple distinct hand gestures—each visually expressive and responsive in VR. These gestures can be combined in countless sequences, creating dynamic, visually rich gesture-dances. Every pattern you perform becomes part of the performance, turning simple hand recognition into a creative movement experience.

  3. 03

    A Rich Library of Music

    Choose from a wide collection of tracks, with a strong focus on Chinese-style music. From gentle traditional melodies to energetic modern blends, the diverse music library lets you enjoy many different atmospheres as you perform your gesture-dance.

  4. 04

    Record Your Own Gesture-Dance

    Create your own choreography with our intuitive in-game recording system. Just perform your gesture-dance in VR, save it as a playable chart, and share it with others to enjoy. No external editors or complicated tools—everything happens seamlessly within the immersive world.
