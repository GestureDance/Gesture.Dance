GAMEPLAY HIGHLIGHTS

Movement first, interface second.

Inspired by rhythm games and contemporary dance, Gesture.Dance focuses on how it feels to move, not how many notes you hit.

Gesture-first Dancing

No step charts, no strict lanes. The game reads your hands in real time and transforms your gestures into responsive visual patterns. You improvise; the system follows, encouraging flow rather than perfection.

In-game mockup
Real-time gesture trails

Space as Instrument

The room around you reacts like an instrument. Sweep upward to lift the scene, cut across to slice through lines of light, and spin to bloom patterns at the edge of your play space.

Environment sketch
Responsive VR stage

Comfortable Short Sessions

Built around 5–10 minute sessions that fit easily into your day. Clear feedback, restrained UI, and gentle pacing let you explore movement without overwhelming visuals or long menus.
